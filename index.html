How we choose our picks

We don’t run lab tests or timed trials. Instead, we start by analyzing long-term owner feedback across multiple retailers and forums, looking for consistent praise or complaints about fit, pilling, shrinking, absorbency, and stitching. Then we cross-check that with published reviews from trusted home and apparel experts, filtering out paid promotions and one-off experiences. We also track what actually breaks: loose seams fading, hoods that don’t stay on, robes that lose their plush after a few cycles. Our final picks are models that have held up well over months of real use according to their owners, offer reliable sizing, and represent a genuine value at their price tier, whether you’re spending very little or splurging.
